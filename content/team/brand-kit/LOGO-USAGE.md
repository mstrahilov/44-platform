# Logo usage

Start with one of the included asset files. This preserves the mark's intended shape and proportions.

Provisional clear space is one quarter of the mark's total height on every side. Provisional minimum size is 32px wide digitally and 10mm wide in print.

Use the white mark on black or another dark background, and the black mark on white or another light background. Social profile images use the white mark on black. Leaving the asset unmodified is the simplest way to keep it recognizable; stretching, cropping, outlining, recoloring, or rebuilding it with typed characters changes the identity.
