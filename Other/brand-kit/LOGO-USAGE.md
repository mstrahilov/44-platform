# Logo usage

Use the approved asset file. Do not recreate the mark with typed characters.

Provisional clear space is one quarter of the mark's total height on every side. Provisional minimum size is 32px wide digitally and 10mm wide in print.

Do not stretch, rotate, crop, outline, redraw, recolor, shadow, glow, animate, merge with another mark, place it over low-contrast imagery, or add a trademark symbol. Use the white mark on black or another dark background. Use the black mark on white, Paper, or another light background. Social profile images use the white mark on black.
